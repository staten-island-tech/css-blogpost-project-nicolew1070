Top 3 Places to Visit

Wildplay Jones Beach

A fun outdoor adventure for those are not scared of heights and up for an challenge. This adventure course includes ziplines, freefall jumps, and many obstable courses to get through. There are 3 levels of obstacles that become harder as you get higher. Make your way up, platform to platform, and enjoy a blast on this course.

Mountain Creek Riding Stable

Located in the Poconos Mountains, enjoy an hour long trail ride with the horses. Whether you are a beginner or experienced rider, this trail ride is a perfect way for you to experience the beauty of the Pocono Mountains. As you ride through the woods, take in the breathtaking scenary and relax with nature.

Mountain Coaster at Camelback Mountain Adventures

Control your own "roller coaster" at the Camelback Mountain Resort. With speeds up to 25 MPH, hold on tight as you embark on a thrilling ride down the mountainside. With many twists and turns, it is sure going to pump your blood up. If this is not your style, you can also choose to go slowly and take in the scenary around you.
